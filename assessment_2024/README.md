# Assessed Coursework for Data Science & Machine Learning for Geoscientists 2024 

### Instructions

1. This coursework is to be completed under exam conditions. Please do not speak to other candidates during the test nor look at your classmate's computer screens.

2. You have 2 hours to complete this assessed coursework. Once you have finished, commit your changes and push those commits to your repository. **Please check that you have successfully pushed your commits - we cannot mark your work if it does not show up on the repository.**

3. We will not mark any commits pushed to your repository after **12pm on the 19th March**. 

4. You are free to use the Internet during the coursework. This includes accessing repos from your previous lectures, Pandas/scikit-learn documentation, and StackOverflow. In cases where you have used the latter, please indicate that you did so and include the link to the StackOverflow question you used.

5. The use of ChatGPT is **NOT** allowed during this coursework.
